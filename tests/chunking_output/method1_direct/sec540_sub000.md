# SPF (UPF) classification according to the Australia/New Zealand Standard AS/NZS 4399:1996 > 소분류 1

- 병합 문단 수: 4
- Proposition: SPF 15 to 24는 좋은 보호를 제공하고, SPF 40 이상은 최고의 보호를 제공합니다.

---

Sun Protection Factor        Protection category       Mean effective UV transmission
SPF 15 to 24*                 good protection                  6.7 to 4.1%
SPF 25 to 39*              very good protection                4.0 to 2.6%
SPF 40 and more, 50+       excellent protection                less than 2.5
